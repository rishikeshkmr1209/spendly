Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. As you use Spendly, we store the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information or any data beyond what is necessary to operate the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and enabling filtering by date or category. We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell or share it with third parties for any commercial purpose.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for infrastructure (such as hosting providers). These providers are bound by their own privacy policies and are only permitted to process your data as necessary to support the operation of our service. We do not integrate with advertising networks or analytics platforms that track you across other websites.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. To delete your account and all associated data, please contact us using the information below. We will process your request within a reasonable time frame.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the contact information available on our website. We are happy to address any concerns you may have.
